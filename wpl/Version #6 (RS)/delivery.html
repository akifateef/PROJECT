Logistics Services

Realizing the potentials of Supply Chain Management in Pakistan, TCS launched its Logistics Wing as a registered company in 2002 as TCS Logistics (Private) Limited.TCS Logistics has 3 divisions, namely:
(a) Overland Express
(b) W&D (Warehousing & Distribution)
(c) Fleet Solutions.

TCS Logistics employs 898 professionals, working day and night to add value to your businesses.

Domestic Express Services

TCS has emerged as the most trusted name for delivering a wide range of documents, packages, parcels and cargoes within Pakistan. Domestic Express Delivery Services include.

- TCS HAZIR
- MMS
- Sentiments Express
- Flyer Express
- Supplementary Services
- Same Day Express
- Overnight Express
- Red Box

TCS Cargo Center

First time in Pakistan, TCS brings you a comprehensive solution for your domestic heavy cargo transportation. TCS Cargo centre at Karachi, Lahore and now in Rawalpindi provides you pickup and delivery of heavy shipments for your household and commercial cargo.
